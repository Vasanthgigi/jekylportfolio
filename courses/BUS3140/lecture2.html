BUS3140 - Customer's Job for your product

Rich Hildred - rhildred@conestogac.on.ca - 519-594-0900

Objectives

  • Define and explain the importance of segmenting the market and defining the target market for a product
  • Define and explain the importance of differentiation in the market
  • Define and explain the importance of being focussed on one market in the early phases of a companies life

Identify, define, and be able to defend the product or service's value proposition, including:

  • Who will pay for their product or service
  • How does the competition succeed
  • How are potential customers currently solving the problem
  • Is the problem large truly enough to drive the customer to purchase
  • Are any developing trends going to cause disruption in coming years

Objectives (cont)

  • Define and explain the process of customer discovery & development
  • Define and quantify customer acquisition costs
  • Describe the process of obtaining feedback from customers, why it is important, and how this feedback can be fed back into business and product development processes
  • Describe how cost drivers, market strategies, ramp up times, and other challenges can affect the starting of the business
