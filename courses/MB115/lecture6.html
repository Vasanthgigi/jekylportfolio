Thinking in Word

Rich Hildred - rhildred@wlu.ca - 519-594-0900

Fundamentally word uses an outline to organize your document to tell a story.

Aristotle defined good drama as storytelling that defined character, created atmosphere, and advanced the action of the plot.

In writing about technology the "plot" is basically what we call the scientific method

In Word we have different pre-defined plots or templates to begin with.

Word comes with a bunch of templates, but a very good source for all kinds of templates is the center for disease control
